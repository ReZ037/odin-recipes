Italian Sunday Sauce!!

This is your red sauce that you will always want to have ready for any family meal! It consits of great basil, orgeano, and tomato flavor!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • basil
  • oregano
  • bay leaves

Steps

  1. Smash your tomatoes and combine with your tomato paste into a croc pot
  2. Combine all your basil, oregano, bay leaves, and garlic all in the croc pot
  3. Let this all combine for 4 hours on low/medium heat
  4. Then serve and enjoy!
