Pizza!!!

This is gonna be your goto homemade pizza! Gone are the days of ordering dominoes or pizza hut! Now you can make delicious pizza in your own kitchen! This is a basic pizza dough with your staple topics of red sauce cheese and pepperoni!!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Bread Flour
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Heat water to around 100 degrees F
  2. combine 1 packet of yeast and 1 tablespoon of sugar
  3. Stir and let sit for 5-10 minutes to let yeast activate
  4. Add 1 cup of flour to a bowl then combine with yeast add any seasonings here you want
  5. Add 2nd cup of flour in slowly and massage dough
  6. Finally once dough is ready coat in oil.
